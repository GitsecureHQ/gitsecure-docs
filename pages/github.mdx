# Adding Gitsecure to Your GitHub Organization

Learn how effortlessly you can integrate Gitsecure into your GitHub organization.

![Gitsecure](/dashhome.png)

### Integrate Gitsecure into Your Organization

To integrate Gitsecure into your GitHub organization, follow these simple steps:

1. Click on the "Integrations" tab on your dashboard.
2. Locate GitHub integration as an option and click on the install button.

![Gitsecure GitHub Integration](/dashintgit.png)

### Select Your Organization

Once redirected to GitHub, select the organization you want to integrate Gitsecure into by clicking on your preferred choice.

![Select GitHub Organization](/git1.png)

### Choose Repositories and Accept Permissions

After selecting the organization, choose whether you want Gitsecure in all your organization's repositories or a specific one. You'll be shown the permissions Gitsecure requires for effective repository monitoring. After selecting repositories and accepting the permissions, proceed by clicking the install button.

![Select Repositories and Accept Permissions](/git2.png)

### View Your Repositories

After installing the Gitsecure GitHub app, you will be redirected back to your Gitsecure dashboard. Here, you'll find a list of repositories that Gitsecure is monitoring.

![View Monitored Repositories](/dashend.png)
