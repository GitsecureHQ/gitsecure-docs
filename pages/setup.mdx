## Setting up Your Workspace on Gitsecure

Learn how easy it is to set up your workspace on Gitsecure.

![Gitsecure](/dashhome.png)

### Integrate Gitsecure into Your Organization

To enable Gitsecure to monitor your code repositories, integrate it into your code hosting platform. Currently, Gitsecure only provides Github integration. To view all available integrations, click on the "Integrations" tab. Learn more about integrating Gitsecure into your GitHub organization [here](/github).

![Gitsecure](/dashintgit.png)

### Add Your Teammates on Gitsecure

For seamless collaboration between teams, create different teams and add team members by clicking on the "Teams" tab.

![Gitsecure](/team.png)

### Update Your Workspace Settings on Gitsecure

Easily update your workspace settings by clicking on the "Settings" tab. Here, you can update your workspace name and perform other actions.

![Gitsecure](/settings.png)
